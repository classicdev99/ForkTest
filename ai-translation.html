AI Translation

The AI-powered translation dApp

AI Translation decentralized application is a cutting-edge technology that allows users to translate text between different languages in a decentralized manner.This means that the application is not controlled by single entity, but rather runs that work together to provide translation services.

One of the unique features of this application is that it accepts cryptocurrency as payment for translation services. This allows users to pay for translations using DXA token.

In addition, It also utilizes external APIs from companies like Microsoft and Grammarly to provide proofreading and editing services for translated text. This ensures that translations are accurate and free of errors, while also providing users with additional tools to improve their writing skills.

Proofreading Machine

One of the advantages of DeplexAI service over any kind of GPT is that AI-translated content is done with grammar correction and spell check by services such as Google, Microsoft, or Grammarly.

Typically, if you use AI translation, you have to proofread your content, which means you have an extra charge for a proofreader service over GPT subscription.

However, with our translation service, you receive error-free text, eliminating the additional costs of subscription services.
