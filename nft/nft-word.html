FAQs

What does mean NFT DXA PFP Optimized?

Match optimized dimensions with the profile image size to ensure word legibility in your profile pictures.

In English, NFTs with up to 6 letters have readability for 40x40 pixels and above, while 7-letter NFTs are readable for at least 48x48 pixels; also, for other languages, you can find size optimization on the NFT detail page. So, you should choose it according to your desired usage.

For example, you cannot use 7-letter NFTs on Twitter (X) or Facebook, but Reddit, Telegram, and WhatsApp support them.

Which languages is NFT DXA accessible?

Approximately half of the word artworks will be in English, and the other half will consist of words from widely used languages: Chinese, Arabic, Spanish, Hindi, Bengali, Russian, Japanese, Vietnamese, Turkish, Korean, French, German, Italian, and Persian.

What restrictions is in word selection?

  • Abbreviations (like OMG, LOL, IDK).
  • Words with a primary intention of promoting racism.
  • Unauthorized Word length (in English, it's less or higher than the allowed characters, or in other languages, it cannot be readable at the smallest size.)
  • Names of individuals (Unless they are mainly used for something other than a person's name, e.g., 'Grace')
  • Country and city names (Unless they are mainly used with a different meaning, such as 'Spartan' which usually means 'minimalist'.)

How will NFT word requests be processed?

  • Submission Review: The DeplexAI team will carefully review each word request. We assess the content and readability of the request to ensure it aligns with the collection's guidelines.
  • Approval: If your word request meets the criteria, it will be available for purchase. As the requester, you can acquire the word at a special rate of 20% below the collection's floor price.
  • Additional Discount: If the story you've provided, along with your word request, is also approved, you'll be eligible for an extra 30% discount to acquire the word at 50% below the collection's floor price.
  • Purchase Period: It's important to note that once your word request is approved, you have up to two weeks to complete the purchase. This timeframe begins from the date you receive the instruction email.
  • Public Availability: If the purchase is not made within the period, the word will be listed for public purchase.
